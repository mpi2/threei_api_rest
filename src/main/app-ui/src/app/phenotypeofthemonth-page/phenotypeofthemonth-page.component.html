Phenotype of the month

Adgrd1

Adhesion G Protein-Coupled Receptor D1

Data at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

Heterozygous fertility: Female infertility
Antinuclear antibodies: Increased level of antinuclear antibodies in serum
Mesenteric Lymph Node - Increased effector γδ cells and KLRG1+ CD8 αβ T cells
DSS colitis: Decreased suspectibility to induced colitis: reduced average histology score
Body composition (DEXA): Decreased bone mineral density in females

Gene information:

  • G-protein couled receptor of unknown function
  • Complex N terminus containing a PTX and a GAIN domain
  • Reported to be required for glioblastoma growth
  • Highly polymorphic in humans: over 9000 SNPs identified (96% in introns)
  • Genetic variation in humans has been linked to height and weight

Phenotype of the month

Clpp

Caseinolytic Mitochondrial Matrix Peptidase Protolytic Subunit

Mice at IMPC

Phenotype Summary:

16 week PBL - decreased γδ T cell percentage in females, increased KLRG1+ NK cells, increased number of B cells and mature B cells in females, increased monocytes in females
Spleen - Increased KLRG1+ NK cells, increased monocytes, reduced NKT cells, reduced CD4+ NKT cells, increased CD4- NKT cells, increased Transitional 2 B cells
MLN - Increased monocytes
Bone Marrow - Increased B cells, reduced granulocytes
DSS - Increased weight loss, increased mortality
Fertility - Infertility in homozygous males and females
Haematology - Increased white blood cell count in females, increased corpuscular colume in females, increased mean corpuscular haemoglobin in females
Plasma Chemistry - Increased total bilirubin, decreased circulating glucose in males, decreased circulating amylase in males, increased circulating alkaline phosphatase in males, increased circulating iron in males
Indirect calorimetry (males tested only) - Decreased oxygen consumption, abnormal energy expenditure
Insulin - Decreased circulating insulin
Body composition - Decreaed bone mineral density, decreased bone mineral content, decreased bone area, decreased body weight, decreased nose to tailbase length in females, reduced estimated total tissue mass, reduced lean mass, reduced fat mass
Auditory Brainstem response - Abnormal Click ABR waveform shape

Gene information:

  • Protease regulating protein levels through degradation
  • Present in most bacteria and in the mitochondria of eukaryotic cells, encoded on chromosome 17 in mice and on chromosome 19 in humans
  • Interacts with AAA+ chaperones which bind and unfold Clp P targets
  • In human, biallelic pathogenic variations of Clpp cause Perrault Syndrome, a condition characterized by sensorineural hearing loss in both genders and ovarian dysfunction in females
