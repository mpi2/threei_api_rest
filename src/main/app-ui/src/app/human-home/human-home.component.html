COVID-IP

Study description

COVID-IP is a fast response study of adaptive immune parameters in patients with COVID19 and their link to clinical outcomes. Whereas we process and interpret the data to inform the clinical teams, we strongly believe that many more researchers in the community may benefit from an early access to our data. To better understand immunological response to the virus and improve treatment, we are making the data available to the community as soon as possible.
