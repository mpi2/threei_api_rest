 The Infection, Immunity and Immunophenoyping project (3i) provides in-depth immunological phenotyping data of over 500 knockout mice that were generated by the Wellcome Sanger Institute. We look at immune cell type distribution in blood, spleen, mesenteric lymph node, bone marrow and ear epidermis, as well as at cytotoxic T cell function in the spleen, ANA in serum and challenge mice with Salmonella, Trichuris, influenza, and DSS-induced colitis.
